💡 Lifestyle Tips for Fertility

😴
Sleep 7–9 Hours
Poor sleep disrupts melatonin and reproductive hormones. Keep a consistent bedtime routine.
🏃
Moderate Exercise
30 min of walking, swimming or yoga 5x per week. Avoid extreme high-intensity training.
🧘
Manage Stress
High cortisol suppresses ovulation. Try daily meditation, deep breathing or journaling.
💧
Stay Hydrated
8–10 glasses per day supports cervical mucus quality and hormonal balance.
🚭
Avoid Smoking
Smoking and alcohol significantly reduce fertility and increase miscarriage risk.
⚖️
Healthy Weight
Both underweight and overweight affect ovulation. Aim for a healthy BMI of 18.5–24.9.
